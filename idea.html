From Idea to Story: Harvey's Last Day in Australia

Where the idea began

This story was inspired by our family moving from Australia to Texas when Harvey was just four months old. Preparing to leave familiar places and travel to the other side of the Earth with a young child made us reflect on how big some changes can feel for families. I wanted to create a story that helps children and adults experience the amazing places, anumals and plants in Austrlia, while also leaving a message that not matter how far away you may be, your family and friends will always be there for you Part way through the two year design process, our daughter Mina was born. This led to discussions about whether to include her in the story, and highlighted how quickly family life can change and become more busy. We spent three years in Texas, travelling to many places that we wouldn't have visited if still in Australia, but the long flight from Houston to Perth was something we were too nervous to dowith two young kids. These reflections helped shape the gentle and reassuring tone of the book that your family is always with you no matter how far apart you might be.

Writing the story

The text was refined to keep the language simple, calm, and easy to read aloud. Each page aims to encourage discussion and help children connect the story to their own experiences of change. The goal was to create a book families can return to when preparing for new beginnings.

Images: Early manuscript draft. Printed pages with edits. Page layout planning.

Main Characters

The illustrations started as relatively simple sketches that captured some of my ideas, such as a red cap, sunglasses and backback. This slowly evolved into a more refined sketch, with colours adjusted to work well with the backgrounds. As the ideas progressed, a more professional digital drawing software was used to begin finalising and illustrations. Minor adjustments continued, such as removing sunglasses and continously assessing the colour palette as the backgrounds changed.

Final book and layout

The final version reflects both the personal journey behind the story and the aim to support children as they experience change. Harvey’s Last Day in Australia is intended to help children feel confident about new experiences, wherever their journey takes them.
