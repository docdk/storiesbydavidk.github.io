The Educator behind the Resources

These resources were designed by an experienced teacher, Milena Kulikowski to help parents and teachers extend the stories through creativity, discussion, and meaningful follow-up activities.

Milena has worked in schools across Adelaide, Perth and the USA. She holds a Bachelor of Education (P-Year 7), a Bachelor of Arts (Physical Education), and a Masters in Educational Leadership.

If you have any recommendations for future resources, please contact us and we can work with you to explore the options. Please also explore the ‘Idea to Story’ section of this site to better understand the creative process involved in creating these books.
