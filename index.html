A place for deeper connection with the stories you read

Stories by David Kulikowski brings together children’s books, family connection, and practical resources for adults who want to make story time more meaningful. The pupose of this website is to provide an easy to navigate repository of books and thier carefully linked resources that Parents and Teachers can easily access and use for more meaningful reading time.

We aim to make the reading ruitine for Parents more fun and educational, and better aligned with the Austrlian Curriculum, but in an easy and safe way. The free resources available for download extend the children's thinking using 'Harvey's Last Day' as a focus text. The resources are based around this book and teach the children about fiction vs non-fiction, rhyming, Austrlaian native flora and fauna, Austrlian geography, onamotapea's, and many more - but in a fun and relaxed environment aimed at improving childrens motivation to beocming lifelong readers and elarners.

We have also crafted a network of meaninigfull resources aimed at Teachers, which can be linked to many Australian Curriculum standards. ALthough the text and resources target children aged 4 - 10, the resources can be differentiated to tailor to specific childrens needs and age groups outside of this range. Additional resources have also been created for younger children (2 - 4) that focus on colouring, memory, pattern recognition and scissor skills. For Teachers interested in detailed lesson plans, cross-curriculum unit plans, and other detailed resources, you can find the link to purchase these at a small cost.

THe list of available resources are the first batch that were chosen to provide maximum impact and connection with the text. There will be additional resources uploaded through time for PArents adn Teachers. We are very open to feedback, so if there is a reosurce that you think would be helpful in the classroom or at home, please dont hesitate to contact us via the Contact page and we can work together to try and meet your needs.

Thank you again for visiting this site and we hope you enjoy exploring its content.
